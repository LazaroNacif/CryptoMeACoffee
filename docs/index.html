WHY CRYPTO ME A COFFEE?

Traditional payment processors take 3-5% fees and add friction. With CryptoMeACoffee, your supporters send USDC directly to your wallet.

The X402 PROTOCOL eliminates gas fees for donors, making crypto donations as easy as traditional payments.

TRY THE WIDGET IN THE CORNER!

GETTING STARTED

Deploy the included backend, add one script tag to your site, and you're live in under 5 minutes:

<script data-name="CMAC-Widget" src="https://unpkg.com/cryptomeacoffee/dist/widget.umd.js" data-wallet="0x..." data-api="https://your-api.com/donate" data-creator-name="Your Name"> </script>

That's it! The widget auto-initializes and renders.
